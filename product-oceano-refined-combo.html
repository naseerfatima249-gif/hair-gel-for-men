Oceano Hair Gel: Ideal for casual, everyday styling. Its medium hold offers flexibility and movement while the ocean-inspired fragrance keeps you fresh. Great for achieving that effortless, natural look.

How to Use

For Strong Hold (Refined):

  1. Apply to damp or dry hair
  2. Use coin-sized amount for short to medium hair
  3. Work through hair and style as desired
  4. Perfect for slicked-back or structured styles

For Medium Hold (Oceano):

  1. Best applied to slightly damp hair
  2. Use smaller amount for natural, textured look
  3. Scrunch or tousle for casual styles
  4. Great for messy, beachy, or swept looks
